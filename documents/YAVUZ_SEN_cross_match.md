## Result

- **Zero confirmed one-to-one matches** between Yavuz Şen's publicly visible stories and the 245.
- The single closest link is a shared **motif** (baker/bread kindness): his *"Ekmek"* vs. your
  **#127 *İsti çörək*** — distinct stories, same theme.

## Why the mismatch

- The **245 are mostly short aphoristic parables** (Aesop, Zhuangzi, Rumi/Sufi, Ottoman
  anecdotes, viral moral forwards) — already traced to those older sources in the origins report.
- His **indexed output is a different sub-genre**: historical biographies, art/painting
  explainers, science trivia, and a few original kindness vignettes.

## Conclusion

- Yavuz Şen is a **prolific curator/reteller in the same broad `hikmetli/kültürel hikâye`
  ecosystem** the collection draws from — a **plausible transmission channel** — but the
  publicly checkable evidence **does not establish him as the source of any specific story**
  in the collection.
- **Not proof of absence:** ~99% of his posts (and everything pre-2023) are not publicly
  indexed. A definitive check requires a **logged-in pass** through his LinkedIn activity, or
  an exported list of his post texts to match precisely against the 245.

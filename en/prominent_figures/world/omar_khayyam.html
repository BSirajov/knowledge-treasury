Omar Khayyam

1048 – 1131

Persia / Iran World scientists

Omar Khayyam About

Life and career

Omar Khayyam (1048–1131) was a Persian mathematician, astronomer, philosopher, and poet. His Rubaiyat is a classic of world literature.

Scholarly and creative work

Khayyam made fundamental contributions to solving cubic equations, the Jalali calendar, and mathematical geometry.

Contribution to society

He is a classic figure of Eastern and Western culture; the Rubaiyat has been translated for millennia.

Murasaki Shikibu About

Life and career

Murasaki Shikibu (c. 973–1014) authored The Tale of Genji, regarded as the world's first novel.

Scholarly and creative work

Her novel depicts Heian court life, psychological depth, and a woman's perspective in a realist style.

Contribution to society

The Tale of Genji is a classic peak of Japanese and world literature, noted in UNESCO's Memory of the World.

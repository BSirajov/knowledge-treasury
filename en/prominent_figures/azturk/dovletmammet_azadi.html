Döwletmämmet Azadi About

Life and career

Döwletmämmet Azadi (1704–1760) was an eighteenth-century Turkmen poet and intellectual. His poetry combines religious-philosophical thought, ethics, and social life.

Scholarly and creative work

Azadi continued the classical poetry tradition in Turkmen, expressing religious teaching together with the life of the people. His lyric poetry is among the early classics of Turkmen literature.

Contribution to society

Azadi played an important role in the formation of Turkmen national literature and set an example for later poets.

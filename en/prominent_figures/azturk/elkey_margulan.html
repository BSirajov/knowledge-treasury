Kazakh Azerbaijani & Turkic heritage

About Alkey Margulan

Life and career

Alkey Margulan is a distinguished figure of Kazakh heritage, widely recognized in the field of Archaeology & etnoqrafiya. This figure's life and work occupy an important place in the scientific, literary, and cultural memory of the Turkic world.

Scholarly and creative work

Alkey Margulan is presented as a figure who contributed to the established traditions of this field and left an enduring mark on both national and broader Turkic cultural memory.

Among the principal areas of this work, particular note is given to activities in Archaeology & Etnoqrafiya; contributions to Enlightenment thought, culture, and scholarly heritage; and to the role in the shared memory of the Turkic world. These examples are the primary indicators of the scholarly, literary, or cultural influence generated in this field.

Contribution to society

Margula's work served to protect the historical heritage in Kazakhstan and establish the narrative of national identity on a scientific basis.

Key works and contributions

  • 1
    Begazy-Dandibay culture studies
    By systematically investigating the ancient archaeological layers of Kazakhstan, he scientifically substantiated the line of early statehood and cultural development.
  • 2
    Studies on Kazakh epic heritage
    Dastan documented national cultural memory through a comparative analysis of oral poetry and ethnographic materials.
  • 3
    Studying the legacy of Chokan Velikhanov
    Kazakh wrote important scientific texts rereading the history of enlightenment based on Velikhanov's work.

Notable aspects of the life and work

⛏️ He became one of the main figures of Kazakh archeology
He gave a strong impetus to the institutional development of archeology in Kazakhstan with his field research and scientific school.
📖 He combined ethnography and folklore
He created a comprehensive model of cultural research with his approach of history, folklore and anthropology working together.

Ahmet Haşim

1884 – 1933

Ottoman Azerbaijani & Turkic heritage

Ahmet Haşim About

Life and career

Ahmet Haşim (1884–1933) was one of the leading poets of the late Ottoman and early Turkish periods. He is known for symbolism and lyrical personality.

Scholarly and creative work

Haşim contributed to the formation of modern lyric in Turkish through collections such as Göl Saatleri, Piyale, and Bize Göre. His poems treat nature, solitude, and the inner world.

Contribution to society

Ahmet Haşim is one of the classic symbols of the modernist current in Turkish literature; his lyric became a model for later poets.

Key works and contributions

  • 1
    Göl Saatleri (Hours of the Lake)
    A classic collection of Symbolist lyric.
  • 2
    Piyale
    Poems on nature and the inner world.
  • 3
    Bize Göre (As We See It)
    An important collection in the development of modern Turkish poetry.

Yusif Mammadaliyev

1905 – 1961

Azerbaijan Azerbaijani & Turkic heritage

About Yusif Mammadaliyev

Life and career

An outstanding representative of Azerbaijani chemistry — a scholar known for petroleum-chemistry research and the development of the Academy of Sciences.

Scholarly and creative work

Yusif Mammadaliyev played a significant role in the development of Azerbaijani petroleum chemistry.

His research was concerned with high-octane fuels and the chemical processing of petroleum products.

Contribution to society

As President of the Azerbaijan Academy of Sciences, he contributed to the formation of national scientific institutions and the institutional growth of chemistry in Azerbaijan.

Key works and contributions

  • 1
    Petroleum chemistry research
    Yusif Mammadaliyev conducted fundamental research on petroleum processing and high-octane fuels, helping to shape the methodology of Azerbaijani petroleum chemistry.
  • 2
    Leadership of the Academy of Sciences
    As President of the Azerbaijan Academy of Sciences from 1947 to 1950, he played a leading role in establishing and coordinating national scientific institutions.
  • 3
    Scientific legacy in chemistry
    His monographs and articles established long-term research directions in the chemical processing of petroleum products, catalysis, and industrial chemistry.

Notable aspects of the life and work

🏛️ President of the Academy of Sciences
In 1947, Yusif Mammadaliyev was elected the first President of the Azerbaijan Academy of Sciences and guided the formation of a national model of scientific governance.
⚗️ Petroleum chemistry school
His research in the Baku petroleum-chemistry milieu strengthened the scientific foundations of high-octane fuels and oil-processing technologies.
